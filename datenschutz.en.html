This website is hosted by Vercel Inc. When the page is loaded, technically necessary data such as the IP address may be processed by the hosting provider. This processing is based on legitimate interests pursuant to Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR to ensure operation of the service.

Vercel stores access logs (including IP address, timestamp, and user agent) for a maximum of 30 days according to standard practice and automatically deletes them afterwards.

The operator does not directly link this data to identifiable individuals.

Technical and Organizational Measures

To protect data processed via this website, the following technical and organizational measures pursuant to Art. 32 GDPR are implemented:

  • Encrypted transmission of all data between browser and server via HTTPS (TLS 1.3)
  • Strict Content Security Policy (CSP) to prevent cross-site scripting and unauthorized third-party scripts
  • No server-side processing of personal data by the operator: all calculations occur exclusively locally in the user's browser
  • No tracking, analytics, or advertising technologies
  • No cookies (features such as market or history storage operate exclusively via the browser's LocalStorage without data transmission)
  • Regular updates of libraries and frameworks in use
  • Hosting with an ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II certified provider (Vercel Inc.)

Since the operator does not actively process personal data, security measures are limited to the infrastructure security mentioned above and the avoidance of any data collection.
